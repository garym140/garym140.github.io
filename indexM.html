Mirror of Truth

Focusing on water management, my team and I created an interactive experience to help elevate the conversation on proper, daily water consumption.

Context

The Experience

The user will be able to turn on and off the faucet knob, ultimately having control over the flow of water.

The potentiometer will detect the rotation of the knob and ths will be connected to the digital screen through an Arduino microcontroller. As water drains, the usr will see how much water of their daily recommended consumption is wasted.

We will be making a water pump system which will allow us to measure the exact amount of water needed for our prototype.

Ideation

Using an Arduino, a plastic sink, a two sided mirror, an ipad, and openframeworks, we were able to mechanically engineer an interaction between a faucet handle and an openframworks sketch.
