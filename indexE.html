ESCAPE

Is a digital interactive story revealing an inside look into hacked emails between 2 friends.
What is privacy?
Does it exist within the digital realm?

Context

You have access to private emails between two individuals. One individual, Sage, wants to escape america due to the intrusive surveillance that exists. Where does she go? Why does she leave? Is she truly able to escape?

As you navigate through this story you are able to learn about the current laws and discussion on Privacy, Technology, and Democracy

Research

This past semester I took a class called 'Dark Data'. This class opened my eyes to the lack of privacy that exists in the world around us.

We studied major nations (US , UK , India , and China) in an attempt to understand the major difference in data privacy protection laws and the lack there of within each nation.

This was also during the time of Cambridge Analytica. So I downloaded my FB data to see what information they collected from me.

Ideation

Creating this digital story took various prototypes.

I researched the best way to display this digital story. So I thought of showing the correspondence between 2 friends through direct messaging on FB, Instagram, or Snapchat. But then I realised that there something charming about the old fashion email correspondence between friends. So I went down the email route and studied the various qualities of gmail and recreated these emails.




For my next iteration, I took away the gmail template because after user testing, some of the feedback was that the gmail look wasn't necesarry. If anything it distracted the reader from the content.




The 'More Info' button wasn't implemented until later on in the process when feedback from both my classmates and professors were all questioning how this story is helping to inform those reading it of the current privacy issues we face in the digital space.

The very last 'creepy' touch in all of this are the live camera feeds in Vietnam that I was able to hack and run 24/7 within this digital story... WILD.
